Stats on Google.com



  • Google.com's domain was registered on September 25 1997.
  • Google is one of the most used search engines, over 1.17 billion searches monthly according to http://expandedramblings.com/
  • Over 50% the amount of searches are on a mobile device.
  • When Google.com went offline for 5 minutes the global internet usage went down 40%.
  • Google was requested to take down 21.5 million URLs in September of 2013
  • 111 billion dollars is the amount of economic activity google's search and advertising tools generated in 2013.
  • 70% of googles wrokforce is male and 30% is female
  • There are an estimate of 25,000 Google engineers and 55,149 Google employees in total.

About the founder of google.com

  • Lawrence Page (born March 26 1973) is an American computer scientist and internet entrepreneur who, with Sergey Brin, is best known as the co-founder of Google.
  • He took on the role of chief executive officer of Google.
  • Today his estimated wealth is $16.7 billion.
  • His goal was to create a search engine that would understand exactly what you mean and give back exactly what you want.
